👾Retro Game Escape

Welcome, Gamer!

You have found yourself trapped in a world beyond what you know. Why are there game machines everywhere? Your goal is to escape by going through a series of challenges to get through each room.

📑 How to Play

  • You must solve the problems of each room that is presented to you to find the escape!
  • Once you find the last escape room... you can finally escape this video game!
  • Most importantly, GOOD LUCK and HAVE FUN!
